General biology and habitat

Chicken are omnivores. [27] In the wild, they often scratch at the soil to search for seeds, insects, and even animals as large as lizards, small snakes,[28] or sometimes young mice. [29]

The average chicken may live for 5-10 years, depending on the breed.[30] The world's oldest known chicken lived 16 years according to Guinness World Records.
